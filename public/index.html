Whats EcoCup?

EcoCup is a forward-thinking bio-degradable coffee cup with a twist. With seeds integrated into the cup, take your ecofriendly habbits to the next level by planting EcoCup after you finish your refreshment.

Why Care?

The deplinishment of renewable resources is ever continuing & switching to renewable is a key turning point across the board. Being not only renewable but also exponentially replisnishing, EcoCup will leave a strong impact on our communites, at home and across the globe.

Sip to Change.

Lets see how it works

  • 1

    Drink

    Enjoy your coffee, drink your favorite morning brew out of EcoCup.

  • 2

    Preperation

    Prepare EcoCup for planting by removing the priming tape & spritzing the seeds with water.

  • 3

    Nurture

    Fill the inside of the cup up with soil, and put the lid on.

  • 4

    Placement

    Place EcoCup on its side on a window seal, with the green strip facing up.

  • 5

    Patience

    Have patience and wait for your plants to germinate!
