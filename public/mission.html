Our Mission

Since the dawn of agriculture over 12,000 years ago, the tree population has declined 46%. This is due to large paper and logging companies that are responsible for 15 billion trees fallen each year.

Eco cup has successfully created a solution to this age-old problem. With our integrated seeds. One biodegradable cup could develop into a fully mature tree that can be used to produce 5000 or more eco-cups.
